Welcome to Morch Client!

Discover a new way to customize your Minecraft experience with Morch, a script loader that brings modding-like capabilities directly into the game.

As one of the pioneers in in-game scripting, Morch allows you to modify the core game mechanics seamlessly without external tools. Utilizing Nashorn, the powerful JavaScript engine, you can write and execute scripts on-the-fly, unleashing your creativity within the Minecraft universe.

Get ready to explore the endless possibilities Morch offers for in-game customization. It's not just modding; it's scripting, right where you play!
